MOVIE REVIEWS

Your guide to the best films

The Shawshank Redemption

1994

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

A masterpiece of hope and friendship. Tim Robbins and Morgan Freeman deliver unforgettable performances in this tale of resilience behind bars.

Inception

2010

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Christopher Nolan bends reality in this mind-bending thriller. Stunning visuals and a complex narrative that rewards multiple viewings.

Parasite

2019

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Bong Joon-ho's brilliant social commentary wrapped in a thrilling narrative. A film that defies genre conventions at every turn.

The Dark Knight

2008

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Heath Ledger's Joker elevates this superhero film to pure cinema. Dark, intense, and morally complex storytelling at its finest.

Pulp Fiction

1994

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Tarantino's non-linear masterpiece redefined independent cinema. Witty dialogue, memorable characters, and iconic scenes throughout.

Everything Everywhere All at Once

2022

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

A wild, emotional ride through the multiverse. Michelle Yeoh shines in this genre-defying exploration of family and existence.

Goodfellas

1990

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Scorsese's unflinching look at mob life is endlessly rewatchable. Fast-paced, stylish, and brutally honest about the American dream.

Spirited Away

2001

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Hayao Miyazaki's enchanting tale is a visual feast. A timeless story of courage and growth wrapped in breathtaking animation.
